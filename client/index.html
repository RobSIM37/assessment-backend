Progress-o-Matic!

The joy of getting things DONE!

The Progress-O-Matic is a website designed to help you progress towards your goals!

Have a task that requires multiple steps to complete? Visit our To-Do Lists and create a new list, cross off the items as you complete them, and celebrate completing the task!

Want to track your progress to a goal? Our Progress Meters can help you visualize it! Trying to reach a savings goal? Set a starting value of 0 and your target to your savings goal! Trying to lose some weight? Set the starting value to your current weight and the target to your desired weight! Depending on the desired direction, the meter will show your progress as a growing or shrinking progress bar!

Want to review all the things you've done using the Progress-O-Matic? Go to the Accomplishments page and look at all the To-Do Lists and Progress Meters you've completed! (Added AUTOMATICALLY!)
